# Annex A – Budget information package

### Purpose

This Annex provides **non-binding orientation** for applicants to the 2026 ČVUT Starting Grant call, including salary ranges and typical cost items.[^1]

## A.1 Indicative salary ranges

| Position | Typical contract  | Indicative range | Amount meaning |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Principal investigator (PI) | Full-time employment | 77,000–112,000 Kč (≈ € 3,100–4,600) monthly  | Gross salary |
| Postdoc | Full-time employment | 68,000–86,000 Kč (≈ €2,700–3,500) monthly  | Gross salary |
| Doctoral student | Scholarship | 36,000–46,000 Kč (≈ €1,500–1,900) monthly  | Net income |
| Student assistants | Agreement for Work Activity | up to 400 Kč (≈ €16) hourly, up to 80 hours monthly  | Gross income |
| Technical or Administrative support | Part-time employment | CZK 42,000–53,000 Kč (≈ €1,700–2,100) monthly   | Gross salary |

## A.2 Typical cost items

Applicants may use the following checklist to ensure common items are considered in budgets:

* **Operations / services:** consumables; small tools; software licenses; cloud/high performance computing resources; data acquisition; sample analysis; prototyping services; publication costs; translation/proofreading.  
* **Travel:** conference registration; travel; accommodation; per diem; visa fees where needed.  
  * European conference: 35,000 Kč (≈ €1,400)   
  * Overseas conference: 75,000 Kč (≈ €3,000)   
* **Child/dependent care:** reimbursed documented costs of formal childcare or dependent-care services required to enable project work by the PI or project team members, up to CZK 150,000 (≈ €6,100) per project year (project total).   
* **Investments:** major equipment purchases or essential research infrastructure with costs exceeding CZK 80,000 (≈ €3,200).  
* **Overheads:** up to **20%** of eligible direct costs, calculated on the direct-cost base **excluding investments**.

## A.3 Relocation package

Where a one-time relocation package is provided under the Call, it is **reimbursed against documented costs**, normally within the **first 12 months** of the project start date, and is funded at **100%** by the Czech Technical University’s Future Fund.

**Relocation package cap:** up to 250,000 Kč (≈ €10,200) per project

### Eligible items may include

* travel and local transport directly related to relocation (PI and dependents, if applicable);  
* moving/shipping of personal effects and essential household items;  
* visa/residence permit fees and mandatory administrative fees;  
* certified translations / notarizations required for relocation administration;  
* temporary accommodation during the initial settling-in period.

### Non-eligible examples

* purchase of personal vehicles;  
* long-term rent beyond the initial settling-in period;  
* luxury goods or discretionary personal purchases.

### Documentation

* invoice/receipt and proof of payment;  
* short justification linking the item to relocation;  
* where relevant: list of dependents covered and dates of relocation.

[^1]:  All amounts in this Annex are indicative only; Czech Crown (Kč) is the reference currency, and any Euro (€) equivalents are approximate, calculated using € 1 \= 24.5 Kč.
